About Us

This website lets you search for recipes and save them into a shopping list. You can also create a meal plan to match your needs.


What's a meal plan?


It's an interface we've created to allow a user to plan their meals ahead of time.


How is a meal plan useful?


We believe it can be important to schedule meals in order to meet specific calory input or to follow certain diets.


What's a shopping list?


It's a list of ingredients you can add to remind you of what you need for a recipe.


Can I add my own recipes?


This was a concept we had come across but opted not to allow users to post recipes in order to best reduce the moderation required.


Is this site secure?


We have no user login requirements or anything that would constitute a security risk.
